Getting Started

Getting started as a Entrepreneur or Developer with DevFindr is an extremely easy 3 step process. If you have any questions about the process feel free to reach out.

Sign Up

In order to get started with us you have to sign up for an account, don't worry it's free to get started as an Entrepreneur or Developer.

Project Prep

Entrepreneur's create project posts, developers submit proposals that include the technologies they can use to bring the Entrepreneur's ideas to life. This workflow ensures both sides know what's going on.

Delivery

Once the Developer completes the project, they are in charge of presenting it. Entrepreneur's pay the remaining balance they owe before delivery.

Safety First

Whether you're an Entrepreneur or Developer your safety comes first. Using industry standard technologies for payments "Stripe" we don't collect or save information such as credit card numbers or banking information. We also protect you from yourself and eachother. As an Entrepreneur or Developer, our intuitive Project Preperation system ensures there are 0 miscommunications between project preperation to development.
